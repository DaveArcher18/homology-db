# Choose the v0.0.1 corpus manifest

## Question

Which exact imported and generated families, unnamed models, torsion examples, duplicate presentations, independent validation subset, minimum counts, and declared omissions satisfy the observable contract honestly?

## Answer

The `0.0.1` selection contract expands to 174 curated conceptual spaces in 15
rollups, including 128 common manifolds and 45 explicitly constructed torsion
examples. Known coincidences such as `M(Z/2,1) = RP^2` are excluded before ID
assignment. Empty-space values and the augmented degree-minus-one convention
are explicit; missing rows never stand for zero.

The post-qualification target is 1,159 presentation-isomorphism classes: 659
finite simplicial Models and 500 witness-certified finite regular-CW Models.
The fixed Stellar v6 reserve contributes 480 in each format, while 174 curated
primary Models and 25 predeclared second presentations supply the remaining
199. Reserve ranks, quotas, source pins, source-file hashes, simpcomp artifact
hashes, duplicate policy, rejection reasons, and release-ledger fields are all
machine-declared. These are release targets, not claims that ingestion or
qualification has already occurred.

Family expansion predicts 138 distinct torsion-bearing `(space, degree)`
pairs across 74 spaces, with primary counts 52, 31, 30, and 28 for primes 2,
3, 5, and 7; 19 prime-power spaces; five multiplicity witnesses; and 102 pairs
outside degree one. The verifier derives these figures from the chosen Moore,
bouquet, nonorientable-surface, projective-space, and lens-space parameters
rather than trusting the declared totals.

The exact G4 grid, disjoint adversarial G5 fixtures, 66-Model independent
validation cohort, 60-common-manifold QA cohort, and 100-prompt acceptance
distribution are fixed before observing results. Generator/oracle independence
and assertion-level provenance must be enumerated in the eventual immutable
release ledger. polyDB remains corroborating metadata; Regina, simplicial sets,
nonregular attaching-map Models, implicit promotion, mass subdivisions,
upstream Homology as canonical truth, and a licensing-policy decision are
declared omissions.
